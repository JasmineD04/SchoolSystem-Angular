360 Performance Analysis System

The 360 Degree performance analysis system will act as a bridge between the teachers and the parents. This system will allow the teachers to fill the data of the students in an orderly manner and generate a performance analysis report on the basis of the data entered which will not consume time unlike the current system where the teachers have to manually enter the data. The parents will be able to view their child's reports and also edit them according to their traits in their homes. The parents will be well aware about their child's behavior in school and can monitor them accordingly. This system will help the universities to function in a more systematic manner by decreasing manual labor and decreased paperwork.The reports generated by our system will provide an insight of the studentsâ€™ performance and personality traits in school and their homes ,which will help them in the near future. This system will ensure that the feedback entered by the teachers is not offensive and will not hurt the sentiments of the students and parents
