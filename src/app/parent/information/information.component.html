About School Page


About- Euro preschool in USA offers classes from pre-primary level through senior kindergarten and maintains a 10:1 student-teacher ratio to facilitate individual attention to each student. Euro Kids Pre-schools in USA introduce the concept of education to children. Play schools in USA train kids to adapt to a new environment. Ideas and concepts like language, counting, alphabets, etc. are taught interactively for a better understanding of children.
